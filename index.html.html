Why should we track calories?

  • Your body counts them, so you need to as well
  • People suck at estimating calorie intake and expenditure
  • It makes monitoring progress easier
  • People who tell you calories don't count are usually promoting a guru system or dogmatic approach
  • It allows for dietary freedom
